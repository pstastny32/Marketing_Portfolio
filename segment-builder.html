The AI Segment Builder.

Building an audience for a campaign used to be slow and technical. So I built a tool that lets a marketer describe the campaign in plain language, set a few parameters, and let AI score every eligible customer and hand back the target list, ready to push to Customer.io. Fewer dependencies, faster sends, and the marketer stays in control.

What it is

A self-serve builder for the email team. Describe the goal, pick the products, set the parameters, and AI returns a scored, ready-to-activate audience.

How I built it

In-house, on top of our data lake and CDP segmentation logic. An AI layer turns plain language and scoring weights into real conditions, with suppression and compliance baked in, then activates straight into Customer.io.

Why it matters

It takes the complex thing and makes it simple, so a non-technical marketer ships without waiting on anyone. Scoring is built to favor real affinity, not a spray-and-pray blast.

Under the hood

How it actually works

1 · Describe

The marketer writes the goal in plain language and picks the promoted products. No query language, no ticket to the data team.

2 · Score

AI scores every eligible customer on affinity, predicted value, and churn risk, using the weights the marketer set.

3 · Filter

Required cohorts, recency, and suppression rules apply automatically, including unsubscribes and the active holdout.

4 · Activate

The reviewed audience pushes straight into Customer.io as a named segment, ready to send.

Preview shown with sample values. The holdout suppression is deliberate: it keeps a clean control so we can measure the campaign's incremental lift, not just its raw sends.
