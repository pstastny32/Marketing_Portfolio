How I work

The through-line

Whether it's a lifecycle, a loyalty motion, or a new platform, it comes back to the same three things.

I build the thing

I love to build, so that's what I did: the lifecycle, the loyalty program, the tools, the reporting. And I stay on them after they launch.

I connect the teams

When we reorganized, our CMO called this a "Swiss army knife" position. My spot has been vital for building relationships across the digital, app, and IT teams and keeping the work moving between them.

I make the data mean something

One of the more interesting parts of my role is running our analytics. I own our Adobe Analytics platform and build the reporting leadership uses. What I care about most is incrementality: did we actually move someone, or would they have done it anyway. Honest signal over vanity metrics.
